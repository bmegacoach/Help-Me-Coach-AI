========================================

Core Ecosystem Overview
-----------------------
The CoachAI ecosystem represents an integrated web3 RWA platform combining education,
marketplace infrastructure, tokenized assets, marketing, sales and distribution smart contract
technology. This knowledge base equips the agent with context across four interconnected
business units: CoachAI Development Fund (capital allocation), CoachAI Tech Camps (talent &
skill development), CAMP DeFi (decentralized marketplace, trading and treasury management),
and CAMP IDL (omnichain sales, marketing and distribution). Each component reinforces the
others through token utility, user progression, and financial infrastructure alignment.

OpenChief is the AI-powered front door to this entire ecosystem — an intelligent C-Suite of
virtual executives that guides users toward the right products, answers domain-specific
questions, and drives revenue through conversational qualification and sales follow-up.

  audited positive performance, providing all ecosystem participants optimized rewards on a

  security suite.
